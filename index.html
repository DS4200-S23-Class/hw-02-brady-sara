Task Abstraction

High Level Task is consume, present. The vis is showing daily maximum temperatures, but clearly highlighting 3 points that it wants the user to see.

Medium action is explore. Even though they point out the last 3 days thats not exactly what the vis is showing. Its more looking at the general trend. So the target points are unknown for the trend and the location is unknown as you do not know exactly what data point it is looking for.

Low action is summarize because the vis is looking at the entire trend throughout the dates.

Target is all data, trends because we are looking at the overall look of the trend of temperatures in Vancouver.

High level action is consume, discover. The visualization presents all the times the daily mean was 20 degrees above normal, so the user is able to look at all that data to gain new insights.

Medium level task is locate. The target of finding days where the mean was 20 degrees above normal is known, but the location of where these days are is unknown.

Low level action is compare. The visualization has both Seattle and Portland and the user can compare the number of times both cities met the mean criteria.

Target is attributes, similarity. The user is going to compare Seattle to Portland, and look at the trend to see if they are similar or different.
